About

In an emergency, timely blood transfusion is very important to save lives. Hexacopters hold a promise for quickly and efficiently delivering medical supplies, such as blood, to remote or inaccessible areas. This report describes the design and development of a hexacopter capable of carrying a 1 kg payload, specifically designed to transport medical blood bags from one hospital to another within a 1 km distance within 10 to 15 minutes.

Design features

Payload capacity: 1 kg
  b. Range: 1 km
  c. Delivery time: 10 to 15 minutes
  d. Using ultrasonic and LiDAR sensors for autonomous navigation
  e. Stable and working Safe in emergency situations


Hexacopters are designed to maximize performance while ensuring stability and safety. The main products are:
- Frame: A lightweight but sturdy frame that can carry loads and protect the outside. We will use carbon fiber as the material due to its high strength and light weight. - Drive System: High - For high-efficiency brushless motors and propellers optimized for lift and durability.

- Flight controller: We will use a flight controller with navigation capabilities and sensor integration support. We will use Pixhawk as our primary flight controller and mission planner for navigation purpose. The battery could provide enough power for a long flight time, we will use Orange Company's 10000mAh Lion battery, which can provide power for up to 1 hour.

- Communication system: We will use RF transmitter and receiver to strengthen the communication link for the remote control and monitor the status of the hexacopter.
